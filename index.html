A Mexican Breakfast Masterpiece: Tradition in Every Bite

Green chilaquiles are the ultimate comfort food, turning simple ingredients into an explosion of flavor. Simmering crispy corn tortillas in a spicy sauce of roasted tomatillos results in a dish that is perfectly tart, slightly spicy, and incredibly satisfying. Topped with fresh cream and fresh herbs, it's a classic tradition that brings the authentic taste of Mexico right to your table in the morning.

Fun Facts & Pro Tips

  • The crunch factor: Add the sauce to the chips at the last minute if you prefer them crispier.
  • Vitamin boost: Fresh tomatillos are packed with vitamin C, making this a surprisingly healthy way to start your day.
  • The perfect pairing: Traditionally enjoyed with a side of refried beans and a hot cup of café de olla.
    • Customized spice: You can substitute jalapeños for serrano peppers if you want a milder version that's suitable for the whole family.
